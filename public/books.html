ALL MUST GO: STORIES

All Must Go is Kevin Sterne's debut story collection about the working class people of Chicago who serve the north side elite. From surreal accidents to heartbreaking arguments, All Must Go delivers bizarre moments of pleasure, of wonder and abandon. For fans of Denis Johnson and Home Improvement. Out Aug 31, 2020 with House of Vlad.
