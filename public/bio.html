Kevin Sterne
Press Writing Books Bio

Kevin Sterne is the author of the story collection All Must Go (House of Vlad, 2020) and the chapbooks From Your Jerry (No Rest Press 2019) and I’ve Done Worse (Long Day Press 2017). His fiction has appeared in SmokeLong Quarterly, The Nervous Breakdown, Maudlin House, Monky Bicycle, and other great magazines. He is the winner of the Phoebe Journal 2020 Fiction Prize.


Kevin lives in Chicago, works in construction, and loves trees. He has a dog named Kodi and drives a truck named Tennessee Jed. Kevin is currently working on a novel about water.
