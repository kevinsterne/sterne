Kevin Sterne is a writer and editor based in Chicago. He's the author of From Your Jerry (No Rest Press, 2019) and I’ve Done Worse (Long Day Press, 2017).

Kevin is the editor in chief of Funny Looking Dog Quarterly, and hosts the monthly Test Literary Series at The Whistler in Logan Square. His fiction has appeared in Maudlin House, SmokeLong Quarterly, Drunk Monkeys, Sobotka, and others. Kevin loves running and trees.
